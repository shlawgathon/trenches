# US Entity

Hardcoded operating profile for the US / CENTCOM actor.

Focus:

- alliance management
- expeditionary basing
- carrier and airpower projection
- domestic political pressure
- market and shipping stability

Primary use in the simulator:

- seed persistent strategic context before live news arrives
- anchor action priors around sanctions, deterrence, and force posture
- give the model fixed geospatial reference points for US reach
